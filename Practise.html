REAL MADRID's 35th triumph.

Today, 30th of april 2022, Real madrid has been crowned as the champions of Laliga. In the last match vs espanyol, Real madrid clinched their 35th title. Rodrygo goes , 19 year wonder kid scored 2 goals followed by Karim Benzema and Isco helped the team to win the game as well as secure their 35th title. With this victory, Real Madrid is the most decorated team in Laliga. This club has had a great history. They have the most champions league titles in european cups. It feels great to win the cup. Thanks a lot to the players as well as all the fans across the globe for constantly supporting the club.
